What's in Vietnam tourism?

Sa Pa is a town in Lao Cai province. This place has a wild but extremely attractive and charming beauty of the mountains and the small roofs of the ethnic people. Sa Pa is a tourist attraction due to its many beautiful scenes and interesting attractions.

Tourist attractions in Sapa attract visitors

Fansipan Peak - Fansipan Legend:
Tourist Area The famous Fansipan peak is a Sapa tourist destination you cannot miss. Located 9km from town, Fansipan communal house is located in the middle of a large and majestic space of nature. With its impressive height, Fansipan peak has excellently registered as the "roof of Indochina".

Muong Hoa valley:
Another highlight when traveling to Sapa is Muong Hoa valley. Located only about 8km from the town, visitors will not take too long to travel to immerse themselves in the large natural space and poetic scenery of Muong Hoa valley. This promises to be a memorable Sapa tourist destination in your trip.
