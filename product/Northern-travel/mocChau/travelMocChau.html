Moc Chau is a large plateau with a temperate monsoon climate. Famous tourist attractions must include Bat Cave, Moc Chau pine forest, Thai Hung waterfall and indispensable tea hills and grasslands in NT Moc Chau town. The district's Moc Chau National Tourism Management Board is implementing and completing eco-tourism areas in Moc Chau town and Moc Chau NT. Currently, there are a number of areas that have been deployed: Dai Yem mining, Bat Cave, ecological lake and pine forest in Ang and Dong Sang villages.
